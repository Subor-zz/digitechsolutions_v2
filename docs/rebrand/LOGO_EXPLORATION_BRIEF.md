# Logo Exploration Brief

## Non-negotiables

The visible name remains **Digitech Solutions**. Explore in black and white first. Every direction must work as full lockup, compact header use, 16 px favicon-derived mark, social avatar, dark/light, one-color print and accessible SVG. Motion is optional enhancement of ≤1 s and never required to identify the brand.

## Direction 1 — Wordmark-led alignment

A precise editorial wordmark with customized alignment/cuts in selected letterforms. A segment may shift from misalignment to a shared baseline in motion, echoing friction-to-flow without compromising reading. Explore hierarchy between “Digitech” and “Solutions”, but never hide the latter in primary lockups. Risk: becoming a generic tech consultancy wordmark; differentiation must survive monochrome and small size.

## Direction 2 — Flow Line monogram

A restrained `D`-led mark in which one interrupted path becomes a continuous route. The mark pairs with the full untouched wordmark and can act as favicon/node motif. Avoid literal plumbing, swoosh or circuit traces. Risk: detail loss at 16 px and over-identification with logistics; test filled and stroked reductions.

## Direction 3 — D/S system mark

A D/S monogram built from a shared grid, boundary and deliberate gap: D represents system boundary, S represents route/change. It can seed diagram geometry without turning every UI element into branding. Risk: ambiguity, similarity to currency/chain marks and unreadable letter collision; require blind recognition testing.

## Evaluation criteria

Score 1–5 with notes: full-name readability; distinctiveness in Dutch IT/consulting context; relevance to controlled modernization; small-size integrity; monochrome/dark/light performance; accessibility and SVG cleanliness; compatibility with editorial engineering art direction; motion potential without dependence; legal/domain/trademark screening; production versatility; avoidance of AI/circuit/swoosh clichés; and ability to coexist with complex diagrams without visual noise.

## Review process and deliverables

Produce three materially different black-and-white systems, each with rationale, full/compact lockups, favicon at 16/32 px, header at realistic size, social avatar, dark/light inversion, one-color print, SVG outline check and a static motion storyboard. Review in context, conduct a five-person recognition/comprehension check, shortlist one direction, then perform legal screening and color/motion refinement. **No final logo is selected in Phase 0.**
